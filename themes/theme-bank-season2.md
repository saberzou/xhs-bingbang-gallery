# Season 2 Theme Bank: "Healing & Empathy" (治愈系)

## How This Works
Based on recent research into high-engagement Xiaohongshu content for young professionals:
- The core focus is on **emotional healing (治愈), self-acceptance, and anti-burnout**.
- The tone is **warm, non-judgmental, and slightly bittersweet/humorous**.
- Each prompt generates an imperfect, hand-drawn Sharpie doodle with 4-pointed sparkle stars (✦).

---

## 1. 允许自己不完美 / 自我接纳 (Permission to Be Imperfect)

1. text: "You don't have to be strong today."
   objects: a tiny knight taking off an oversized helmet, sitting down to rest, the helmet has a cute little face looking at him
   caption: 世界给的子弹够多了，不要再做自己的狙击手。今天允许自己不勇敢。

2. text: "It's okay to do nothing."
   objects: a cute battery icon with little arms and legs, laying flat on its back, fast asleep under a tiny blanket
   caption: 允许自己有一段没有明确产出的时间。休息，也是一种进步。

3. text: "Be your own soft place."
   objects: a person hugging a giant, squishy marshmallow character, both have small content smiles
   caption: 你不需要时刻强大，做大人累了，回来做个孩子吧。

4. text: "Broken things are beautiful too."
   objects: a broken teacup that has been glued back together, with tiny cute flowers growing out of the cracks
   caption: 不完美也是完美的一部分。可以慢，可以平凡，但不可自弃。

5. text: "Stop fighting yourself."
   objects: a small character shaking hands with their own solid black shadow, both smiling
   caption: 和自己和解，是人生最重要的必修课。

---

## 2. 慢下来的勇气 / 反内卷 (The Courage to Slow Down)

6. text: "No need to rush."
   objects: a cute snail carrying a little house on its back, overtaking a broken, exhausted alarm clock
   caption: 你不必追赶任何人的时间表。人在享受的时候总想着慢慢来，不要快。

7. text: "Late bloomers still bloom."
   objects: a tiny, happy sprout wearing a tiny party hat, standing next to a fully grown flower
   caption: 不是所有的等待都是浪费，有些花就是开得晚一点。

8. text: "Breathe in, breathe out."
   objects: a cute little cloud character gently blowing a small pinwheel
   caption: 停下来深呼吸。慢慢来吧，一切都会变好的。

9. text: "The world can wait."
   objects: a small character sitting inside a giant, cozy coffee mug, reading a tiny book
   caption: 地球少转一天也没关系。给自己放个假。

10. text: "Detour is part of the journey."
    objects: a curvy, winding road with a cute little car happily bouncing along it, ignoring the straight path
    caption: 走弯路也是风景，人生没有白走的路。

---

## 3. 悄悄努力的人被看见 (Quiet Persistence)

11. text: "I see you trying."
    objects: a tiny, solid black star shining brightly in a corner, with a larger moon character looking at it proudly
    caption: 你默默努力的样子，其实很耀眼。

12. text: "Clap for yourself."
    objects: a pair of detached hands clapping for a tiny, tired character who is sitting down
    caption: 没有人鼓掌的时候，自己给自己鼓掌也算数。

13. text: "You've come so far."
    objects: a character looking back at a long trail of tiny footprints they just made
    caption: 回头看看，你比你以为的，走得更远了。

14. text: "Keep your light alive."
    objects: two hands gently shielding a tiny, flickering candle from the wind
    caption: 保护好你内心的光，总有一天它会照亮别人。

15. text: "Small wins count."
    objects: a tiny character holding up a very small, cute trophy cup with immense pride
    caption: 即使是微不足道的胜利，也值得庆祝。

---

## 4. 回到内心的安全感 (Inner Safe Harbor)

16. text: "Come back to yourself."
    objects: a tiny character walking through a door shaped like a glowing heart
    caption: 每个人的内心深处都有一处最柔软的地方，它需要被发现，被呵护。

17. text: "Take off your armor."
    objects: a cute little turtle stepping out of a heavy, metal shell, stretching comfortably
    caption: 你不用假装坚强，在这里可以卸下盔甲。

18. text: "Safe from the storm."
    objects: a giant, friendly mushroom sheltering a tiny, sleeping bug from heavy rain
    caption: 外面的风很大，但你可以先回家。

19. text: "No explanations needed."
    objects: a character wrapped tightly in a blanket burrito, with a "Do Not Disturb" sign hanging on it
    caption: 不必向任何人解释你的疲惫。

20. text: "Listen to your quiet voice."
    objects: a character sitting cross-legged, wearing headphones connected directly to their own heart
    caption: 外面的声音太吵了，听听自己心里的声音。

---

## 5. 苦中作乐 / 丧萌幽默 (Bittersweet Humor)

21. text: "Professionally tired."
    objects: a coffee cup character completely melted into a puddle on the floor, holding up a peace sign
    caption: 查完支付宝余额，我这颗悬着的心终于不跳了。今天也是假装清醒的一天。

22. text: "This is fine."
    objects: a cute stick figure sitting calmly at a desk, while a solid black fire burns behind them
    caption: 打工，千万别入戏太深。生活虐我千百遍，我待生活如初恋（才怪）。

23. text: "Task failed successfully."
    objects: a 404 error window character wearing a tiny party hat and throwing confetti
    caption: 既然搞砸了，那就顺便庆祝一下吧。

24. text: "Out of office."
    objects: an open laptop that a cat has completely taken over and gone to sleep on
    caption: 今天的工作进度：帮猫主子按摩。

25. text: "Trying my best (barely)."
    objects: a tiny character lifting a very small, cute dumbbell and sweating profusely
    caption: 别人都去撞南墙了，我去修南墙一定很赚钱。

## Rotation Rule
Week pattern: 自我接纳→丧萌幽默→反内卷→悄悄努力→安全感→丧萌幽默→自我接纳
