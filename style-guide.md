# @BingBang Style Guide

## Visual Identity

### Core Aesthetic
- **Medium:** Black & white doodle art — a mix of bold outlines AND solid black fills
- **Style:** Cute icon/sticker aesthetic. Not minimal line sketches. Objects have personality — think "friendly character design meets doodle"
- **Line weight:** Thick, confident strokes. Marker/bold pen feel, not wispy or thin
- **Fills:** Use solid black fills for contrast (vinyl records, clock faces, sunglasses, stripes). The interplay of black-filled and white-outlined shapes creates visual punch

### Character & Personality
- **Cute faces on everything.** Objects have tiny dot eyes and small smiles/expressions. Envelopes, stars, moons, surfboards — everything is a character
- **Sparkle stars (✦):** 4-pointed diamond sparkles scattered as decoration. Appears in EVERY image. 3-6 sparkles per composition. This is the signature motif
- **Small details:** Tiny dots, motion lines, freckle dots on objects. These add life without adding complexity

### Composition
- **Center-clustered, floating.** Objects grouped in the center of the frame, not grounded on any surface
- **No background.** Pure white. The objects float in space
- **Square format (1:1)** — NOT 3:4
- **Text at bottom:** Short English phrase below the illustration, separated by clear white space

### What This Is NOT
- ❌ Thin wispy line drawings
- ❌ Detailed illustration or realism
- ❌ Minimal/empty compositions
- ❌ Gray tones or gradients
- ❌ Grounded scenes with floors/horizons (exception: when the scene calls for it, like a beach)
- ❌ Chinese text on the image

## Text on Image
- **Language:** English only
- **Style:** Bold italic hand-lettered look
- **Format:** Short punchy phrase, ends with a period
- **Placement:** Centered at bottom of image
- **Tone:** Motivational, relatable, slightly cheeky. The kind of thing you'd put on a sticker or tote bag
- **Examples:** "You can do it tomorrow." / "Stay original." / "Better days are coming." / "Wish you dreams come true."

## Caption Voice (XHS post caption)
- **Language:** Chinese (Simplified), occasionally bilingual
- **Tone:** Reflective but not pretentious. Short observations about life, design, creativity
- **Length:** 1-3 sentences max. The drawing speaks, the words whisper
- **Style:** Like a note you'd scribble in the margin of a book

## Content Pillars (rotate daily)
1. **设计感悟** — Design observations, principles made visual
2. **生活碎片** — Small life moments, everyday poetry
3. **创意灵感** — Creative sparks, "what if" ideas sketched out
4. **文字涂鸦** — English word art with doodle objects
5. **情绪速写** — Emotional sketches, feelings as cute characters

## Image Specs
- **Aspect ratio:** 1:1 (square)
- **Resolution:** at least 1080x1080px
- **Style prompt anchors:** See generate.py for current prompt templates

## What Makes It Work
The magic is cute objects with personality. Everything has a face. Solid black fills create contrast and visual weight. The sparkle stars tie every image into a consistent series. The English text adds a universal, sticker-like quality. It's friendly, share-worthy, and instantly recognizable.
