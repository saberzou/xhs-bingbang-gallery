# Character Bible — Sage & Andy

## Sage (熊脸 Bear)
- **Species:** Bear
- **Head:** Large rounded rectangle, wider than tall, flat top/bottom with soft radius corners
- **Ears:** Two small semi-circular nubs on outer top corners
- **Eyes:** Two tiny solid black dots, set very wide apart near face edges
- **Nose/Mouth:** Minimalist "I" shape — tiny horizontal dash (nose), short vertical line, tiny horizontal dash (mouth)
- **Body:** Chibi/stubby proportions, body height ≈ head height, short tubular limbs, rounded nub hands
- **Clothing:** Plain light/white t-shirt (sometimes with small black heart ♥ on chest), loose trousers, simple rounded shoes
- **Personality:** Gentle, thoughtful, quiet strength. The listener. Steady and grounded. Gives hugs.

## Andy (猫脸 Cat)
- **Species:** Cat
- **Head:** Same rounded rectangle as Sage
- **Forehead:** THREE thick vertical parallel black stripes (tabby pattern) — KEY distinguishing feature
- **Ears:** Slightly pointed triangular ears on top corners (vs Sage's round nubs)
- **Eyes/Mouth:** Identical to Sage — wide-set dots, "I" mouth
- **Body:** Same chibi proportions as Sage
- **Clothing:** Contrasts Sage — black/dark t-shirt (sometimes with small black diamond ◆ on chest), simple pants, rounded shoes
- **Personality:** Adventurous, curious, energetic. The doer. Drags Sage into fun situations. Surfs, plays guitar, tries new things.

## Art Style Spec
- **Line quality:** Clean digital monoline, constant thickness, no tapering/pressure variation
- **Color:** Pure black and white only. No gray, no color.
- **Fill:** Solid black for accents (clothing, small icons, eyes). Hollow outlines for everything else.
- **Composition:** Centered, flat 2D perspective, single ground line, massive negative space buffer
- **Background:** Pure white, no textures
- **Typography:** Hand-drawn all-caps sans-serif with rounded terminals, centered at bottom, generous spacing between lines
- **Visual elements:**
  - 4-pointed hollow diamond sparkles (✦) — 2-4 per image
  - Short horizontal parallel motion lines for movement
  - Simplified props drawn with same line weight as characters
- **Aspect ratio:** 1:1 square (matching XHS optimal)

## Dynamic
Sage and Andy are best friends. They complement each other — Sage is the calm anchor, Andy is the spark. Together they navigate daily life with warmth and humor. Their world is simple, quiet, and positive.
